Vanqo Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

Vanqo does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Your financial information never leaves your device.

The Short Version

  • No data collected
  • No data shared with anyone
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No accounts or sign-ups
  • No network requests
  • All data stored locally on your device

Data Storage

All information you enter into Vanqo (debts, payments, paycheck details, progress) is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is not accessible to Kynoss Studios or any third party.

Data Collection

Vanqo collects no data whatsoever. We do not use analytics SDKs, crash reporting services, advertising frameworks, or any third-party code that collects information about you or your usage of the app.

Network Communication

Vanqo makes no network requests. The app functions entirely offline. The only network communication that occurs is through Apple's StoreKit framework when you make or restore an in-app purchase, which is handled entirely by Apple according to their privacy practices.

Bank Connections

Vanqo does not connect to your bank, credit cards, or any financial institution. All debt and payment information is entered manually by you.

Third-Party Services

Vanqo contains no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or external services. The only external integration is Apple's StoreKit for processing in-app purchases.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by using the "Reset All Data" option in the app's Settings. Uninstalling the app also removes all stored data from your device.

Children's Privacy

Vanqo does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The app contains no accounts, no tracking, and no data transmission.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update this page with the new date. Given that our core principle is "no data collection," we do not anticipate meaningful changes.
