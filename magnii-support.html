Frequently Asked Questions

Does Magnii upload my photos anywhere?
No. Magnii processes everything entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework and camera pipeline. No photos, inspection sessions, or reports are ever uploaded, transmitted, or shared. We have no servers to receive them.
Why does Magnii need camera access?
Magnii uses your iPhone's camera for live magnification and image capture. The camera feed is processed on-device for text recognition, object identification, and measurement. No camera data is ever stored beyond the images you explicitly capture within the app.
How does text recognition work?
Magnii uses Apple's Vision framework to recognize text on captured images. This runs entirely on your device — no text is ever sent to a server. Text recognition works best on still captures with good lighting and contrast.
How does measurement work?
Place a standard calibration card (like a credit card or the included printable card) in the frame alongside the object you want to measure. Magnii detects the card, calculates the real-world scale, and measures the object relative to the card. Accuracy is approximately ±2-5% for flat objects.
What is the difference between free and paid?
Magnii offers a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. After the trial, magnification and basic capture remain free. Text recognition, measurement, annotation, PDF export, and the session library require a one-time $4.99 Pro purchase. There are no subscriptions.
Does Magnii work without an internet connection?
Yes. All magnification, text recognition, measurement, annotation, and PDF export features work entirely offline. Magnii makes no network requests.
What data does Magnii store?
Magnii stores your inspection sessions locally on your device: captured images, annotations, extracted text, measurements, and session metadata. This data is stored using Apple's SwiftData framework, protected by iOS encryption. No data is ever transmitted off your device.
What happens to my data if I delete the app?
All Magnii data is stored locally on your device. When you delete the app, all inspection sessions, captured images, and associated data are permanently removed by iOS. There is nothing to delete from any server because no data is ever transmitted off your device.
What iOS version is required?
Magnii requires iOS 18.1 or later and is designed for iPhone.
Do I need a special lens for macro photography?
No special lens is required. Magnii works with any dual-camera iPhone using the ultra-wide camera for close-up magnification. A clip-on macro lens can enhance extreme close-ups on non-Pro devices but is entirely optional.
