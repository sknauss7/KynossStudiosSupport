Getting Started

Enveloop helps you budget using the envelope method. Here's how to get started:

  1. Complete Onboarding: Enter your paycheck amount, frequency, and account balance
  2. Add Your Bills: Tap the + button in the Bills tab to add recurring expenses
  3. Process Paychecks: On payday, tap "Process Paycheck" to automatically allocate funds
  4. Track Progress: Watch your envelopes fill up over time
  5. Pay Bills: When it's time to pay, swipe left on a bill and tap "Pay"

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the envelope system work?

Enveloop calculates how much you need to set aside from each paycheck for every bill. For example, if you have a $600 car insurance bill due in 3 months and you get paid biweekly, Enveloop divides $600 by the 6 remaining paychecks = $100 per paycheck.

Can I manually adjust envelope amounts?

Yes! Tap any bill to view details, then tap the "Edit" button next to Envelope Progress to manually adjust the allocated amount.

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We never collect, transmit, or have access to your information.

Tap the bill, then tap "Edit" next to the envelope progress to reduce the allocated amount. Enveloop will adjust future allocations automatically.

Can I change my paycheck information?

Yes! Go to Settings and update your paycheck amount, frequency, or next pay date at any time.

What pay frequencies are supported?

Enveloop supports Weekly, Biweekly (every 2 weeks), Semi-Monthly (twice a month), and Monthly pay schedules.

What bill frequencies are supported?

You can add bills that recur Weekly, Biweekly, Monthly, Quarterly, Semi-Annually, or Annually.
