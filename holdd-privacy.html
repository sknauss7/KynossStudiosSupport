# Privacy Policy for Holdd **Last Updated: June 5, 2026** ## Introduction Holdd ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our approach to data collection and usage. ## Data Collection **We collect ZERO data.** Holdd stores everything locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We have no servers, no backend, no analytics, no tracking, and absolutely no access to your data. Holdd never connects to your bank and has no account system. ## Information Stored on Your Device The following information is stored locally on your device: - Items you put on hold (title, price you enter, the product link or source you shared, category, the date captured, and your chosen cooling-off window) - Your decisions on each item (skipped or bought) and the date decided - Your running "Leak plugged" total of skipped items - App settings and preferences **This data is yours.** Holdd has no account system and no servers of its own. Your data syncs across your own Apple devices through your private iCloud (see the iCloud Sync section below). ## iCloud Sync Holdd uses Apple's CloudKit to sync your held items across the Apple devices signed into your iCloud account. This data lives in your **private** iCloud database — it is governed by your Apple ID and Apple's iCloud security, and **Holdd has no server and cannot see, access, or recover it**. There is no shared or public data and nothing is ever synced to anyone else. If you are not signed into iCloud, Holdd simply keeps everything on your device with no sync. ## On-Device Intelligence Holdd's reflection prompts and weekly reflections are generated by Apple's on-device Foundation Models (Apple Intelligence). This processing happens entirely on your iPhone. The text of your held items is never sent to us or to any third party. ## Share Extension When you share a product page to Holdd, the link and page title are passed from the share sheet to the app through a private App Group container on your device. This is a local hand-off between Holdd and its own share extension — nothing is transmitted off your device. **Holdd does not read or capture the price from a web page; you enter the price yourself.** ## Notifications If you allow notifications, Holdd schedules a single local "Still want it?" reminder for when an item's cooling-off window ends. These notifications are scheduled on your device and are not sent through any server. ## Data Sharing and Third Parties We do not share any data because we do not have access to any data. Holdd does not use: - Analytics services - Advertising networks - Crash reporting services - Social media integrations - Any third-party SDKs or libraries that collect data ## In-App Purchase Holdd is free to download. A one-time in-app purchase unlocks Holdd Pro (an unlimited shelf, full Leak-Plugged history, weekly reflections, and per-category cooling-off rules). Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework. We do not collect or store any payment information — all billing is managed by Apple through your Apple ID. ## Data Security Since all data is stored locally on your device and protected by iOS's security measures: - Your data is protected by your device passcode/biometrics - Data is automatically encrypted by iOS when your device is locked - We cannot access, recover, or reset your data ## Your Rights and Control You have complete control over your data: - **View**: All your data is visible within the app - **Edit**: You can modify or delete any held item at any time - **Delete**: Remove individual items, or delete the app to erase all data from your device ## Children's Privacy Holdd does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13, because we don't collect any information at all. ## Changes to This Policy If we ever change how the app handles data, we will: - Update this privacy policy - Update the "Last Updated" date - Notify users through an app update description We will never retroactively change our core privacy principle: your data stays on your device. ## California Privacy Rights (CCPA) Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have certain rights. However, since Holdd does not collect any personal information, these provisions do not apply. ## European Privacy Rights (GDPR) Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), EU residents have certain rights. However, since Holdd does not process any personal data, these provisions do not apply. ## Summary **Your spending is your business. Period.** Holdd was built from the ground up with privacy as the foundation — no bank login, no account, no servers. The whole point is to help you pause before you spend, entirely on your own device. --- *Holdd — pause before you buy, without a bank login.*
