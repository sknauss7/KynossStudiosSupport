Riffl

Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 23, 2026

Overview

Riffl is built by Kynoss Studios. Your privacy is simple: we collect no data. No tracking, no accounts, no analytics, no network requests. Everything stays on your device.

Information We Collect

None. Riffl does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. The app makes zero network requests.

Data Stored on Your Device

Riffl stores the following data locally on your device to provide core game functionality:

  • Game progress — your current puzzle state (tile assignments, reassignment count, completion status)
  • Player statistics — games played, games won, current streak, longest streak, and star distribution
  • Preferences — whether you have completed the onboarding tutorial

This data is stored in your device's local storage (UserDefaults) and is never transmitted off your device. It cannot be accessed by Kynoss Studios or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Riffl uses no third-party services. There are no analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, crash reporters, or external APIs of any kind.

Sharing

Riffl includes an optional share feature that lets you share your puzzle result using the iOS share sheet. The shared text contains only your star rating and an emoji pattern — no personal information. Sharing is entirely initiated by you and handled by the operating system.

Device Permissions

Riffl requests no device permissions. It does not access your camera, microphone, location, contacts, photos, health data, or any other sensitive system resource.

Children's Privacy

Riffl does not collect any data from anyone, including children. The app contains no user-generated content, social features, or in-app purchases.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, deleting the app permanently removes all Riffl data. There is no server-side data to request deletion of.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. Because Riffl collects no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
