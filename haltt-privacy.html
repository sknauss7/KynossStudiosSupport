1. Overview

Haltt ("the App") is developed and published by Kynoss Studios ("we," "us," or "our"). Haltt is an impulse spending prevention app for iPhone (iOS 26 and later) that helps you pause before impulse purchases by analyzing screenshots of products, running cooling-off timers, and tracking money saved.

This Privacy Policy explains what data Haltt accesses, how that data is processed, and your rights regarding your information. We are committed to transparency and to protecting your privacy.

Haltt operates entirely on your device. We do not operate servers, databases, or cloud services. We do not create user accounts. We have no mechanism to collect, receive, view, or store your personal data. The data Haltt works with exists solely on your iPhone, under your control.

2. Screenshot & Image Processing

Haltt never accesses your photo library.

Haltt only processes screenshots and images that you explicitly share with it — either through the Share Sheet extension or by importing within the app. No background scanning, no library access, no automatic collection.

How Screenshots Are Used

  • When you share a screenshot with Haltt, the image is processed on your device using Apple's Vision framework to extract text (product names, prices, retailer information).
  • The extracted text is then analyzed by Apple's on-device Foundation Models framework to classify the purchase.
  • The original screenshot image is not permanently stored by Haltt. Only the extracted text data (product name, price) and analysis results are saved.
  • No screenshot data is ever uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any server or third party.

What We Store From Screenshots

Data Stored Purpose Contains Image Data?
Product name (extracted text) Display in purchase history No
Price (extracted number) Savings calculation and tracking No
Want/need classification AI analysis result No
Timer status and outcome Tracking whether you resisted or purchased No

3. On-Device AI Processing

Haltt does not send any data to third-party AI services.

All AI processing uses Apple's built-in on-device frameworks. No purchase data, screenshots, or personal information is ever transmitted to OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, or any other external AI provider.

Haltt uses two Apple frameworks for on-device intelligence:

  • Vision framework: Reads text from screenshots (optical character recognition) to extract product names and prices. Runs entirely on your device's Neural Engine.
  • Foundation Models framework: Classifies purchases as wants or needs and provides brief reasoning. This model runs entirely on your device — no data is sent to any server, including Apple's.

On devices without Apple Intelligence support, Haltt falls back to a rule-based classification system that requires no AI model and works entirely with local logic.

No AI processing involves any external service, API call, or network request. All intelligence is computed locally.

4. Notifications

Haltt uses standard iOS local notifications (not push notifications) for:

  • Cooling-off timer expiration reminders
  • Streak maintenance reminders
  • Weekly savings digest (optional)

These notifications are scheduled locally on your device. No notification data is transmitted to any server. You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings.

5. Data Storage & On-Device Processing

All data processing occurs entirely on your iPhone. Haltt has no servers, no cloud infrastructure, no databases, and no backend of any kind. We literally cannot access your data because we have no system to receive it.

Purchase data and app state are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is protected by:

  • iOS Data Protection (encrypted at rest, tied to your device passcode)
  • App sandboxing (only Haltt can access its own data store)
  • Standard iOS security architecture

Haltt does not use:

  • iCloud or any cloud storage
  • Any third-party SDKs, frameworks, or libraries
  • Analytics services (no Firebase, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude, etc.)
  • Advertising SDKs or ad networks
  • Crash reporting services
  • Any form of user tracking or fingerprinting

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Retention Policy

  • Purchase records, timer history, savings data, and achievement progress are retained on your device until you delete them or delete the app.
  • No data has a forced expiration — you control how long it stays.

Deletion

  • Delete the app: Deleting Haltt from your device permanently removes all data. Your screenshots in the Photos app remain unaffected.
  • In-app data management: You can clear individual items or all data from within the app.
  • There is no server-side data to delete, no account to close, and no request process needed. You have full and immediate control.

7. Data Sharing & Third Parties

Haltt does not share your data with anyone.

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your data with third parties.
  • We do not transmit your data off your device.
  • We do not use third-party SDKs that could collect data.
  • We do not serve advertisements.
  • We do not engage in data mining or user profiling.
  • We do not share data with parent companies, subsidiaries, or related entities (Kynoss Studios is an independent sole developer).

Haltt makes no network requests whatsoever. The app functions entirely offline.

8. Data Security

Haltt relies on Apple's built-in security infrastructure to protect your data:

  • Encryption at rest: All local data is protected by iOS Data Protection, which encrypts data using keys tied to your device passcode.
  • App sandboxing: iOS prevents other apps from accessing Haltt's data store.
  • No network exposure: Because Haltt does not transmit any data over any network, there is no risk of data interception, server breaches, or unauthorized remote access.
  • No accounts: There are no credentials to steal, no passwords to breach, and no accounts to compromise.

9. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Haltt is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Because Haltt does not collect, transmit, or store personal information on any server, and because all data remains solely on the user's device, Haltt does not create the data collection risks that COPPA is designed to address. Nonetheless:

  • Haltt does not collect personal information from any user, including children.
  • Haltt does not require or support account creation.
  • Haltt contains no advertising or social features.
  • Haltt does not transmit any data to Kynoss Studios or any third party.

If you are a parent or guardian and have questions about this policy, please contact us at kynossstudios@gmail.com.

10. Your Rights Under GDPR (European Economic Area)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) provides you with specific rights regarding your personal data. We respect these rights, even though Haltt's on-device architecture means we do not act as a data controller or processor of your personal data in the traditional sense — because your data never reaches us.

Legal Basis for Processing

To the extent any on-device processing constitutes "processing" under the GDPR, the legal basis is:

  • Consent: You explicitly choose which screenshots to share with Haltt. You may stop sharing at any time.
  • Legitimate interest: Processing your data on your own device to provide the app's functionality directly to you.

Your Rights

  • Right of access: All data is stored on your device and accessible to you at all times within the app.
  • Right to rectification: You can edit or update any purchase record within the app.
  • Right to erasure: You can delete all data by deleting the app, or clear data within the app's settings.
  • Right to restrict processing: You can stop sharing screenshots at any time without affecting other app functionality.
  • Right to data portability: Pro users can export their data from within the app.
  • Right to object: You can stop all data processing by not using the app or deleting it.

Data Transfers

Haltt does not transfer personal data outside your device. No cross-border data transfers occur because no data is transmitted to any server, anywhere.

Data Protection Officer

Due to the nature of our data processing (entirely on-device, no data collection), we are not required to appoint a Data Protection Officer. For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at kynossstudios@gmail.com.

11. Your Rights Under CCPA/CPRA (California)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) provides you with specific rights.

Information We Collect

Under CCPA/CPRA definitions, Haltt processes the following categories of information on your device only:

  • Screenshots you explicitly share (processed for text extraction, not stored as images)
  • Purchase information you enter manually (product names, prices)

However, because all processing occurs on your device and we never collect, receive, or access this information, Kynoss Studios does not "collect" personal information as defined by the CCPA/CPRA.

Your Rights

  • Right to know: This Privacy Policy describes all data the app processes. We do not collect additional data beyond what is described here.
  • Right to delete: Delete the app to remove all data, or use in-app data management features.
  • Right to opt out of sale: We do not sell your personal information. We never have and never will.
  • Right to opt out of sharing: We do not share your personal information with third parties for cross-context behavioral advertising or any other purpose.
  • Right to non-discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your privacy rights.

"Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information"

Haltt does not sell or share personal information. Because no data is transmitted from your device to us or any third party, there is no sale or sharing to opt out of. We fully comply with the CCPA/CPRA without requiring any user action.

12. Apple Platform Disclosures

In compliance with Apple's App Store Review Guidelines and platform requirements:

Vision Framework (OCR)

  • Haltt uses Apple's Vision framework to perform optical character recognition (OCR) on screenshots you share.
  • Vision processing runs entirely on-device using the Neural Engine.
  • No image data is transmitted to Apple or any third party.

Foundation Models Framework (On-Device AI)

  • Haltt uses Apple's Foundation Models framework for purchase classification on supported devices.
  • This framework runs a small language model entirely on your device.
  • No prompts, inputs, or outputs are transmitted to Apple's servers or any third party.
  • Haltt does not use any third-party AI services (no OpenAI, no Google, no Anthropic).

Required Reason APIs

Haltt uses only Apple's first-party frameworks and does not access any APIs that require declared reasons beyond those already disclosed in this policy and in the app's App Store privacy nutrition label.

App Tracking Transparency

Haltt does not track users across apps or websites owned by other companies. The App Tracking Transparency framework is not used because no tracking occurs.

App Privacy Nutrition Label

Haltt's App Store privacy nutrition label accurately reflects that the app does not collect data linked to user identity and does not track users.
