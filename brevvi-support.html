Frequently Asked Questions

Does Brevvi send my recordings to a server?
No. All transcription and AI summarization happen entirely on your iPhone using Apple's on-device frameworks. Your audio, transcripts, and summaries never leave your device. Brevvi has no servers, no cloud storage, and no user accounts.
What devices does Brevvi support?
Brevvi requires an iPhone 15 Pro or later running iOS 26 or later. The on-device transcription and AI summarization features require the A17 Pro chip (or later) and Apple's SpeechAnalyzer and Foundation Models frameworks.
Does Brevvi record audio?
Yes. When you start a meeting recording, Brevvi captures audio and saves it locally on your device as an AAC file. This audio is used for on-device transcription and is available for your review. Audio files are stored only on your iPhone and are never uploaded or transmitted anywhere.
What does Brevvi Pro include?
The free version includes full recording and transcription with up to 3 AI summaries per week and 7 days of meeting history. Brevvi Pro ($14.99 one-time purchase) unlocks unlimited AI summaries and action items, unlimited meeting history, full-text search across all meetings, and export to Markdown and PDF.
Is there a subscription?
No. Brevvi Pro is a one-time purchase. There are no recurring charges, no subscription fees, and no hidden costs. Pay once, use forever.
How do I manage the microphone permission?
Brevvi requires microphone access to record meetings. You can manage this permission at any time in Settings > Brevvi. If you revoke microphone access, you will not be able to start new recordings, but your existing meetings and transcripts remain accessible.
What happens to my data if I delete the app?
All Brevvi data is stored locally on your device. When you delete the app, all associated data — recordings, transcripts, summaries, and settings — is permanently removed by iOS. There is nothing to delete from any server because no data is ever transmitted off your device.
Can Brevvi identify different speakers?
Speaker identification is not available in the current version. This feature is planned for a future update.
