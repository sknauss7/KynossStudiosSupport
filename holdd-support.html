Getting Started

Holdd helps you catch an impulse buy before you spend. Here's the loop:

  1. Park it. About to buy something? Share the product page to Holdd, or tap "Hold something" and add it yourself.
  2. Cool off. Holdd asks two calm questions and starts a cooling-off timer. Your item waits on the Held shelf.
  3. Decide with a clear head. When the timer's up, skip it or buy it — no guilt either way.
  4. Watch the leak close. Every skip adds its price to your growing "Leak plugged" total.

Free & Holdd Pro

Holdd is free to use, including the full hold-and-cool-off loop and up to 5 items on hold at once. Holdd Pro is a one-time purchase (no subscription) that unlocks an unlimited Held shelf, your full lifetime Leak-Plugged history, weekly on-device reflections, and per-category cooling-off rules.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add something to hold?

Two ways: while browsing, tap the Share button and choose Holdd, or open Holdd and tap "Hold something" to add an item by hand. You'll confirm the price and pick a cooling-off window.

Why do I have to type the price?

Holdd never reads or scrapes prices from web pages — that keeps it private and reliable. You enter the price yourself so your "Leak plugged" total is always accurate.

What is the cooling-off timer?

It's a waiting period you choose (from a few hours up to a few days) before you decide. When it ends, Holdd sends one gentle "Still want it?" reminder. There's no nagging and no second reminder.

Does Holdd connect to my bank?

No — never. Holdd has no bank login and no account. It works entirely on your device. It's about the buys you stop, not the transactions you've already made.

Where is my data stored?

On your device, and synced across your own Apple devices through your private iCloud. Holdd has no servers and collects zero data — we never see it. The reflection prompts are generated on-device by Apple Intelligence. If you're not signed into iCloud, everything simply stays on your device.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open Holdd, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchase." Your Holdd Pro unlock is linked to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device.

The reflection prompts aren't appearing — why?

The on-device reflection features require an Apple Intelligence–capable iPhone. The hold flow, cooling-off timer, and Leak-Plugged total work on any supported device.

How do I delete my data?

Delete any held item by swiping it away, or delete the app to erase everything from your device.
