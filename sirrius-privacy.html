Sirrius Privacy Policy

Pet Health — Private & Smart

Effective Date: April 13, 2026

The short version: Your pet's health records live on your iPhone. We have no servers, no accounts, no third-party analytics SDKs, no ads, and no cross-app tracking. The app emits Apple's own on-device OSLog and MetricKit signposts for paywall-flow diagnostics — these stay on your device unless you opt into Apple's system-wide diagnostic sharing. The only data that ever leaves your device is (1) optional household sync via your personal iCloud and (2) the text of questions you send from the Pro AI chat tab, which are passed through OpenAI's API for processing and are not stored, logged for training, or shared with us.

1. Overview

Sirrius ("the App") is a privacy-first pet health tracker for iPhone. It helps you manage pet profiles, health records, medication reminders, vet visits, vaccinations, weight tracking, symptom journals, and food safety checking.

Sirrius is not a veterinary tool. It does not diagnose conditions, provide medical advice, or replace professional veterinary guidance. Always consult your veterinarian for health concerns about your pet.

We (Kynoss Studios) operate no servers, databases, or cloud services ourselves. We do not create user accounts. We have no mechanism to collect, receive, view, or store your personal data. Your pet records live on your device (and, optionally, in your personal iCloud for household sharing). The Pro AI chat tab sends the text of your questions through OpenAI's API for processing — this is described in detail in Section 3.

2. Pet Health Data

Sirrius stores the following types of data, all entered by you:

  • Pet profiles: Name, species, breed, birthday, weight, photo
  • Health records: Vet visits, vaccinations, medical notes
  • Medications: Names, dosages, schedules, administration logs
  • Symptom journal: Free-text symptom entries with dates and severity
  • Feeding logs: Feeding times and amounts
  • Weight history: Weight measurements over time

All data is entered manually by you. Sirrius does not access your camera roll, contacts, microphone, location, or any other device sensor. The only photo access is when you explicitly choose to add a pet profile photo.

3. AI Chat Processing

Pet health records stay on your device. The only AI-related data that leaves your iPhone is the text of questions you type (or dictate) into the Pro AI chat tab. That text is sent over HTTPS to OpenAI's API, processed in memory to generate a response, and is not stored, retained, or used to train models. We never see it — we have no servers.

What the AI chat sends to OpenAI

When a Pro subscriber sends a message in the Chat tab, Sirrius calls OpenAI's API (the gpt-4o-mini model) directly over HTTPS. The request contains:

  • The exact text of your question (for example, "Can Luna have grapes?" or "Summarize Mochi's recent symptoms").
  • A minimal pet context block assembled on your device — pet name, species, and age — so the response is relevant. This context is not your full record; it is a short summary built at request time and discarded when the request completes.
  • Recent turns of your in-app conversation so multi-turn answers stay coherent. These turns live only in memory during the session and are not persisted anywhere by us.

The request does not contain your vet visit records, medications, weight history, photos, symptom entries, or any other stored pet data. None of that leaves your device.

What OpenAI does with your queries

OpenAI is used as a processor under its standard API terms. Per those terms, API requests are not used to train OpenAI's models and are not retained for training. OpenAI's privacy practices are described at openai.com/policies/privacy-policy.

OpenAI may transiently retain API request data for abuse monitoring for up to 30 days before deletion. Sirrius does not pass any account, device, user, or advertising identifier to OpenAI — requests are authenticated with the app's developer API key, not a per-user identifier, so individual users are not identifiable at the OpenAI layer.

Free tier

The free tier of Sirrius does not call the OpenAI API at all. Free users' chat messages are handled entirely on-device by keyword matching and the local food safety database. If you never subscribe to Pro, no text you type in Sirrius ever leaves your device.

Food & plant safety checker

The food and plant safety checker works for all users — free and Pro — and runs entirely offline against a curated local database bundled with the app. Food safety queries never leave your device.

Stored records and journals

Your symptom journal entries, vet records, medications, weights, photos, and pet profiles remain in local storage (plus iCloud if you enable household sharing). We do not transmit any stored record to OpenAI, to any other third party, or to any server we operate.

Disabling AI entirely

To stop all AI API calls, simply don't subscribe to Pro, or cancel an existing Pro subscription. The app remains fully functional for record-keeping, safety checks, reminders, and household sharing without any subscription.

4. iCloud & Household Sharing

Sirrius offers optional household sharing so family members can view and update the same pet records.

  • Sharing uses Apple's CloudKit (CKShare) framework. Data syncs through your personal iCloud account, not through our servers.
  • You control who has access. Only people you explicitly invite can see your pet data.
  • Shared data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple's iCloud infrastructure.
  • We have no access to your iCloud data. CloudKit sharing is a direct peer-to-peer connection between iCloud accounts managed by Apple.
  • User preferences (display settings, notification preferences) are stored privately and never shared.

Household sharing is entirely optional. Sirrius works fully as a single-user app without iCloud.

5. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your iPhone. We have no servers, no cloud infrastructure, no databases, and no backend of any kind. We literally cannot access your data because we have no system to receive it.

Data is stored using Apple's Core Data framework, protected by:

  • iOS Data Protection (encrypted at rest, tied to your device passcode)
  • App sandboxing (only Sirrius can access its own data)
  • Standard iOS security architecture

Sirrius does not use:

  • Any third-party SDKs, frameworks, or libraries
  • Analytics services (no Firebase, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude)
  • Advertising SDKs or ad networks
  • Crash reporting services
  • Any form of user tracking or fingerprinting

6. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Delete the app: Deleting Sirrius permanently removes all local data. No data exists anywhere else (unless you enabled household sharing, in which case shared records remain in iCloud for other household members).
  • In-app management: You can delete individual pets, records, or entries at any time within the app.
  • There is no server-side data to delete, no account to close, and no request process needed.

7. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your data, share your data with advertisers, or use any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. Two narrowly-scoped third parties are involved, and only at your explicit opt-in:

  • Apple iCloud (optional): If you enable household sharing, your pet records sync through your own iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit framework. This is a peer-to-peer connection between iCloud accounts managed by Apple — we have no access. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI (Pro chat only): If you subscribe to Sirrius Pro and use the AI Chat tab, the text of your chat messages plus a minimal pet context block is sent over HTTPS to OpenAI's API for processing. OpenAI does not train on API data and does not retain it for training. See Section 3 for the exact scope of what is sent. Governed by OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers.
  • We do not use third-party SDKs that could collect data.
  • We do not serve advertisements.
  • We do not engage in data mining or profiling.
  • We do not transmit your stored pet records (vet visits, medications, weights, photos, symptom entries) to any third party, ever.

8. Data Security

  • Encryption at rest: All local data is protected by iOS Data Protection.
  • App sandboxing: iOS prevents other apps from accessing Sirrius's data.
  • No network exposure: No personal data is transmitted over any network (CloudKit sync is encrypted end-to-end by Apple).
  • No accounts: No credentials to steal, no passwords to breach.

9. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Sirrius is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because Sirrius does not collect or transmit any data to any server, it does not create the data collection risks COPPA addresses.

If you are a parent or guardian with concerns, contact us at kynossstudios@gmail.com.

10. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are in the European Economic Area, the GDPR provides you with specific rights. Because your data never reaches us, you have full control:

  • Access: All data is on your device and accessible within the app.
  • Rectification: Edit any record within the app.
  • Erasure: Delete individual records or the entire app.
  • Restrict processing: Disable household sharing or AI features at any time.
  • Portability: Your data is stored locally on your device.
  • Object: Stop all processing by deleting the app.

No cross-border data transfers occur because no data is transmitted to any server.

11. Your Rights Under CCPA/CPRA

If you are a California resident:

  • Right to know: This policy describes all data the app processes.
  • Right to delete: Delete the app or individual records.
  • Right to opt out of sale: We do not sell your personal information. Never have, never will.
  • Right to non-discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising privacy rights.

12. Apple Platform Disclosures

App Privacy Nutrition Label

Sirrius declares "No Data Collected" on the App Store. Under Apple's App Privacy definitions, data that is only processed in memory on your device (or in a third-party processor's ephemeral memory) and is not used to track you, build a profile, or persist beyond a single request does not count as "collected." This label covers:

  • Kynoss Studios (us): We collect nothing. We have no servers.
  • OpenAI (Pro chat processor): API calls are used only to generate the current response; data is not linked to your identity (no account, no device ID, no advertising ID is passed), is not used to train models, and is not retained beyond OpenAI's short-term abuse-monitoring window.
  • Apple CloudKit (optional household sharing): Runs under your iCloud account; governed by Apple's own policies.

CloudKit

If you enable household sharing, data syncs through Apple's CloudKit under your iCloud account. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

App Tracking Transparency

Sirrius does not track users across apps or websites, does not pass any identifier to any third party, and does not use the App Tracking Transparency framework because no tracking occurs.
