Frequently Asked Questions

How does Thresh work?
When you meet someone in person, you share a Thresh profile instead of your number. Your private dealbreaker criteria — the standards you set for things like age, height, smoking, kids, and distance — run quietly and show you whether that person is a match. They only ever see match or miss, never the criteria themselves.
What are the two modes?
Cold approach is the zero-prep flow for the moment two strangers meet — share your profile in seconds, no setup. Bouncer mode lets you keep a personal code anywhere people find you; applicants reach you only if they clear your criteria first.
Does the other person need the app?
To submit an application and actually reach you, yes — they install Thresh (free) and apply. An App Clip lets someone preview a shared profile before installing.
What does vouching do?
Friends can vouch for you. When your profile reaches someone, it carries an aggregate trust signal — for example "Vouched by 3+" — so you arrive as more than a face. Vouches are free for everyone and expire after 24 months unless re-confirmed.
What's free and what's paid?
You can hold a profile, give and receive vouches, and take part in a handoff when the other person is subscribed — all free. A subscription means your handoffs always complete, you can run your own gate on your terms anytime, and you get bouncer mode, advanced filters, and screening history. Thresh offers a monthly plan, an annual plan, and a one-time lifetime purchase.
Can I report or block someone?
Yes. Every profile can be reported, and any user can be blocked. Blocking is immediate — a blocked user can no longer reach you. Reports are reviewed within 24 hours.
Are my dealbreaker criteria ever shown to anyone?
No. Your criteria are stored on your device and used only to compute a result. Nobody else ever sees the standards you set — or which one they failed. They see only match or miss.
Does Thresh collect any data or track me?
Thresh has no operator-managed servers, no analytics SDKs, no third-party trackers, and no ads. Your shared profile, applications, and vouches are written to Apple's CloudKit (the same iCloud infrastructure your Photos and Notes use) so the handoff works — but records carry only an Apple-managed opaque identifier, never your name or email. See our Privacy Policy for the full disclosure.
How do I delete my account?
Thresh includes an in-app account deletion option. It removes your on-device data and deletes your profile, applications, and vouches from CloudKit. Allow up to 30 days for full propagation. You can also email us to request deletion.
Does Thresh need an internet connection?
An internet connection is needed to share a profile, submit or receive applications, and sync vouches. There is no Kynoss server — only Apple's CloudKit.
What iOS version is required?
Thresh requires iOS 26 or later and is designed for iPhone.

Privacy

Privacy is fundamental to Thresh's design — your dealbreaker criteria are never shown to anyone, and Kynoss Studios cannot see your name or email. For the complete disclosure of what's stored where, please read our Privacy Policy.
