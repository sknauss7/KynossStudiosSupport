The Promise

Privacy isn't a feature. It's the architecture.

Every app follows the same rules

  • All data processing happens on your device — not "mostly" on-device, entirely
  • No user accounts. No sign-ups. No email collection. Open the app and start using it
  • No analytics SDKs, no tracking pixels, no fingerprinting. We don't know who you are
  • AI features use Apple's on-device models. Your data never hits a server
  • One-time purchases or optional subscriptions. Free tiers are fully functional, not crippled demos
  • Delete the app and everything goes with it. There's nothing on our end to delete
