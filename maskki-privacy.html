1. Overview

Maskki ("the App") is developed and published by Kynoss Studios ("we," "us," or "our"). Maskki is a privacy-first web browser for iPhone (iOS 26 and later) that blocks trackers, explains privacy threats, and summarizes webpages using on-device AI.

This Privacy Policy explains what data Maskki accesses, how that data is processed, and your rights regarding your information. We are committed to transparency and to protecting your privacy.

Maskki operates entirely on your device. We do not operate servers, databases, or cloud services. We do not create user accounts. We have no mechanism to collect, receive, view, or store your personal data. The data Maskki works with exists solely on your iPhone, under your control.

2. Browsing Data

All browsing data stays on your device. Maskki stores browsing history, bookmarks, and settings locally. None of this data is transmitted to Kynoss Studios or any third party.

What Maskki Stores Locally

Data Stored Purpose Shared Externally?
Browsing history History view, back/forward navigation No
Bookmarks Quick access to saved pages No
Content blocking statistics Display tracker/ad counts blocked No
AI-generated summaries Cached page summaries for quick access No
App settings and preferences Personalized browsing experience No
Pro purchase status Unlock Pro features Apple (StoreKit) manages transactions

3. Content Blocking

Maskki blocks trackers, ads, and privacy-invasive scripts using content blocking rules that are bundled directly in the app.

  • No server requests for block lists. All content blocking rules are included in the app bundle. Maskki does not download, fetch, or update block lists from any external server.
  • Blocking happens locally. Content is filtered on your device before it loads, using Apple's WebKit content blocking APIs.
  • No data about blocked content is transmitted. Statistics about what was blocked are stored locally and never leave your device.

4. On-Device AI Processing

Maskki uses Apple Foundation Models to power AI features including privacy threat explanations and webpage summarization.

  • All AI processing runs entirely on your device using Apple's on-device Foundation Models framework.
  • No webpage content, text, or browsing data is sent to any server for AI processing.
  • AI features require iPhone 15 Pro or later (A17 Pro chip or newer). On older devices, core browsing and content blocking work normally without AI features.
  • Apple Foundation Models are a built-in iOS component. No third-party AI services, APIs, or cloud models are used.

5. Network Activity

The only network activity is your actual web browsing. When you navigate to a website, Maskki fetches that website's content — just like any browser. Beyond that, Maskki makes no network requests to Kynoss Studios or any third party.

Specifically, Maskki does not make network requests for:

  • Analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reporting
  • Block list updates (lists are bundled in the app)
  • AI model downloads or cloud AI processing
  • User account verification or authentication
  • Advertising or ad targeting
  • Any communication with Kynoss Studios servers (we have no servers)

7. Data Storage & On-Device Processing

All data processing occurs entirely on your iPhone. Maskki has no servers, no cloud infrastructure, no databases, and no backend of any kind. We literally cannot access your data because we have no system to receive it.

Browsing data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is protected by:

  • iOS Data Protection (encrypted at rest, tied to your device passcode)
  • App sandboxing (only Maskki can access its own data store)
  • Standard iOS security architecture

Maskki does not use:

  • iCloud or any cloud storage
  • Any third-party SDKs, frameworks, or libraries
  • Analytics services (no Firebase, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude, etc.)
  • Advertising SDKs or ad networks
  • Crash reporting services
  • Any form of user tracking or fingerprinting

8. Data Retention & Deletion

Retention Policy

  • Browsing data (history, bookmarks, settings, cached AI summaries) is retained on your device until you clear it within the app or delete the app.
  • You can clear browsing data at any time from within the app.

Deletion

  • Clear browsing data: Maskki provides controls to clear your browsing history, bookmarks, and cached data at any time.
  • Delete the app: Deleting Maskki from your device permanently removes all local browsing data, history, bookmarks, settings, and cached AI summaries.
  • There is no server-side data to delete, no account to close, and no request process needed. You have full and immediate control.

9. Data Sharing & Third Parties

Maskki does not share your data with anyone.

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your data with third parties.
  • We do not transmit your data off your device (beyond your own web browsing requests).
  • We do not use third-party SDKs that could collect data.
  • We do not serve advertisements.
  • We do not engage in data mining or user profiling.
  • We do not share data with parent companies, subsidiaries, or related entities (Kynoss Studios is an independent sole developer).

10. Data Security

Maskki relies on Apple's built-in security infrastructure to protect your data:

  • Encryption at rest: All local data is protected by iOS Data Protection, which encrypts data using keys tied to your device passcode.
  • App sandboxing: iOS prevents other apps from accessing Maskki's data store.
  • No network exposure: Because Maskki does not transmit personal data over any network, there is no risk of data interception, server breaches, or unauthorized remote access to your browsing data.
  • No accounts: There are no credentials to steal, no passwords to breach, and no accounts to compromise.
  • Content blocking: Maskki actively blocks trackers and scripts that could compromise your privacy on the websites you visit.

11. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Maskki is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Because Maskki does not collect, transmit, or store personal information on any server, and because all data remains solely on the user's device, Maskki does not create the data collection risks that COPPA is designed to address. Nonetheless:

  • Maskki does not collect personal information from any user, including children.
  • Maskki does not require or support account creation.
  • Maskki contains no advertising, social features, or in-app communication.
  • Maskki does not transmit any data to Kynoss Studios or any third party.

If you are a parent or guardian and have questions about this policy, please contact us at kynossstudios@gmail.com.

12. Your Rights Under GDPR (European Economic Area)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) provides you with specific rights regarding your personal data. We respect these rights, even though Maskki's on-device architecture means we do not act as a data controller or processor of your personal data in the traditional sense — because your data never reaches us.

Legal Basis for Processing

To the extent any on-device processing constitutes "processing" under the GDPR, the legal basis is:

  • Legitimate interest: Processing your data on your own device to provide the app's browsing functionality directly to you.

Your Rights

  • Right of access: All data is stored on your device and accessible to you at all times within the app.
  • Right to erasure: You can clear browsing data within the app or delete all data by deleting the app.
  • Right to restrict processing: You can stop all data processing by closing the app or deleting it.
  • Right to data portability: Your data is stored locally on your device and is accessible to you.
  • Right to object: You can stop all data processing by deleting the app.

Data Transfers

Maskki does not transfer personal data outside your device. No cross-border data transfers by Kynoss Studios occur.

Data Protection Officer

Due to the nature of our data processing (entirely on-device, no data collection), we are not required to appoint a Data Protection Officer. For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at kynossstudios@gmail.com.

13. Your Rights Under CCPA/CPRA (California)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) provides you with specific rights.

Information We Collect

Under CCPA/CPRA definitions, Maskki processes the following categories of information on your device only:

  • Browsing activity data (history, bookmarks, content blocking statistics)

However, because all processing occurs on your device and we never collect, receive, or access this information, Kynoss Studios does not "collect" personal information as defined by the CCPA/CPRA.

Your Rights

  • Right to know: This Privacy Policy describes all data the app processes. We do not collect additional data beyond what is described here.
  • Right to delete: Clear browsing data within the app, or delete the app to remove all local data.
  • Right to opt out of sale: We do not sell your personal information. We never have and never will.
  • Right to opt out of sharing: We do not share your personal information with third parties for cross-context behavioral advertising or any other purpose.
  • Right to non-discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your privacy rights.

"Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information"

Maskki does not sell or share personal information. Because no data is transmitted from your device to us or any third party, there is no sale or sharing to opt out of. We fully comply with the CCPA/CPRA without requiring any user action.

14. Apple Platform Disclosures

In compliance with Apple's App Store Review Guidelines and platform requirements:

WebKit Content Blocking

  • Maskki uses Apple's WebKit content blocking APIs to filter trackers, ads, and privacy-invasive scripts.
  • Content blocking rules are bundled in the app and processed locally by WebKit.
  • No browsing data is shared with Kynoss Studios through the content blocking system.

Foundation Models (On-Device AI)

  • Maskki uses Apple's Foundation Models framework for on-device AI features (privacy explanations, page summaries).
  • All AI inference runs on the device's Neural Engine. No data is sent to Apple or any third party for AI processing.
  • AI features are available only on supported hardware (A17 Pro and later).

StoreKit 2 (In-App Purchases)

  • Maskki uses StoreKit 2 to offer a one-time "Maskki Pro" in-app purchase.
  • All transaction processing is handled by Apple. Kynoss Studios does not receive, store, or have access to payment information.
  • Purchase status is verified locally using Apple's on-device Transaction API.

Required Reason APIs

Maskki uses only Apple's first-party frameworks and does not access any APIs that require declared reasons beyond those already disclosed in this policy and in the app's App Store privacy nutrition label.

App Tracking Transparency

Maskki does not track users across apps or websites owned by other companies. The App Tracking Transparency framework is not used because no tracking occurs.

App Privacy Nutrition Label

Maskki's App Store privacy nutrition label accurately reflects that the app does not collect data linked to user identity and does not track users. The label declares: "No Data Collected."
