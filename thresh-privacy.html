1. Overview

Thresh ("the App") is developed and published by Kynoss Studios ("we," "us," or "our"). Thresh is an in-person dating-handoff utility for iPhone (iOS 26 and later). When you meet someone in real life, you can share a structured profile instead of your phone number; a private set of "dealbreaker" criteria you define screens the people who approach you and shows you — not them — whether they are a match. Friends can vouch for a person as a trust signal.

This Privacy Policy explains exactly what data Thresh handles, where it is stored, and what Kynoss Studios can and cannot see. Because Thresh uses Apple's CloudKit infrastructure, more data leaves your device than for an offline-only app — but Kynoss Studios still operates no servers, stores no data, and cannot see any personally identifying information.

Kynoss Studios is a sole-developer studio. We run no servers, databases, analytics pipelines, or marketing infrastructure. The CloudKit-resident data Thresh creates lives in Apple's CloudKit container; Apple operates that infrastructure on our behalf, and we never receive or store identifiable copies of it.

3. CloudKit

Thresh uses Apple's CloudKit public database — world-readable storage so that a profile you hand off can be viewed, and an application can reach the person it was sent to. Records are tagged with Apple-managed opaque iCloud identifiers, never with names, emails, or other directly identifying information.

Apple operates CloudKit on Kynoss Studios' behalf. Kynoss has dashboard access to query record counts and schema, but cannot read individual user records — Apple's CloudKit access controls require the requesting party to be the record creator, which Kynoss is not.

Thresh stores nothing in your private CloudKit container at this version.

4. Your Private Dealbreaker Criteria

Your dealbreaker criteria — the standards you set for things such as age, height, smoking, kids, and distance preferences — are the most sensitive data in Thresh, and they are treated accordingly:

  • Your criteria are stored on your device. They are used only to compute a match-or-miss result for the people you screen.
  • The criteria themselves are never displayed to anyone else. Another person only ever sees whether they were a match or a miss — never the values you set, and never which criterion they failed.
  • Kynoss Studios never receives your dealbreaker criteria.

5. Information About Other People

Thresh is a tool for an interaction between two people, so the App necessarily handles some information about people other than you:

  • When someone shares a profile with you, you receive their structured profile (first name, photo, age range, short bio) — the information they chose to share.
  • When someone applies to reach you, you receive the attributes they submitted for screening, together with the match-or-miss result.
  • You are responsible for handling other people's shared information respectfully and lawfully. Thresh does not permit posting content, allegations, or claims about non-consenting third parties.

6. Push Notifications (APNs)

Thresh sends push notifications via Apple Push Notification service (APNs) — for example, to let you know that a new application has cleared your criteria and is waiting for you. Push notifications are opt-in via the standard iOS prompt and can be disabled at any time in iOS Settings. Apple operates APNs; Kynoss receives no notification-delivery telemetry beyond Apple's standard error responses, and no notification content is logged.

7. Reporting & Blocking

Thresh includes tools to report a profile and to block a user. Blocking is enforced on your device; a blocked user can no longer reach you. When you submit a report, the reported profile reference and your report reason are sent to Kynoss Studios so we can review it and act on objectionable content or behavior. We aim to act on reports within 24 hours.

8. Data Retention, Deletion & Account Deletion

Retention

  • On-device data is retained until you delete it in-app or delete the App.
  • CloudKit records are retained for the operational lifetime of the service. Vouch records expire automatically 24 months after creation unless re-confirmed.

Deletion & in-app account deletion

  • In-app account deletion: Thresh provides an in-app option to delete your account. This removes your on-device data and deletes your Profile, your Application records, and Vouch records you created from CloudKit. Allow up to 30 days for full propagation.
  • Delete the App: removes all local data on your device. CloudKit records remain until you use in-app account deletion or the contact path below.
  • By request: contact kynossstudios@gmail.com and we will delete your CloudKit records. Allow up to 30 days.

9. Data Sharing & Third Parties

Kynoss Studios does not share your data with anyone. The only third party involved in Thresh is Apple, which provides the platform infrastructure (CloudKit, APNs, StoreKit) the App relies on. Apple's handling of this data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your data with marketing, advertising, or analytics third parties.
  • We use no third-party SDKs, frameworks, or libraries that could collect data.
  • We do not serve advertisements.
  • We do not engage in cross-app tracking, fingerprinting, or user profiling.

10. Data Security

  • Encryption at rest: local data is protected by iOS Data Protection (encryption tied to your device passcode).
  • Encryption in transit: all CloudKit, APNs, and StoreKit traffic uses Apple's TLS-encrypted transport.
  • App sandboxing: iOS prevents other apps from accessing Thresh's local data store.
  • Public CloudKit access controls: records are world-readable but only writable by their creator (iCloud-authenticated).
  • No Kynoss-operated infrastructure: Kynoss runs no servers, so there are no Kynoss credentials to breach and no Kynoss systems to compromise.

11. Children's Privacy

Thresh is intended for adults and is rated 17+. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age required to use the App. If you believe a minor has used Thresh, contact us at kynossstudios@gmail.com and we will remove the associated data.

12. Your Rights Under GDPR (European Economic Area)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the General Data Protection Regulation provides specific rights regarding your personal data. Most data subject to GDPR is held under your iCloud account by Apple — not by Kynoss Studios. Where Kynoss acts as a data controller (the public CloudKit records tagged with your opaque identifier), we honor the rights below.

Legal Basis for Processing

  • Legitimate interest: processing CloudKit records to deliver the App's core functionality.
  • Consent: sharing a profile, applying, vouching, and push notifications are all opt-in actions you take.

Your Rights

  • Right of access: your on-device data is visible to you in the App; we will provide a dump of your CloudKit records on request.
  • Right to erasure: use in-app account deletion or the contact path in Section 8.
  • Right to restrict / object: stop sharing profiles and applications, or stop using Thresh and request deletion.
  • Right to data portability: CloudKit records are exportable on request.

Apple operates CloudKit globally and is GDPR-compliant. Due to the small scale of Kynoss Studios, we are not required to appoint a Data Protection Officer; for privacy inquiries contact kynossstudios@gmail.com.

13. Your Rights Under CCPA/CPRA (California)

If you are a California resident, the CCPA as amended by the CPRA provides specific rights.

  • Information collected: profile fields, application attributes, and vouch records, tagged with Apple-managed opaque identifiers. No name, email, postal address, phone number, or IP address is collected by Kynoss Studios.
  • Right to know & right to delete: described throughout this policy; see Section 8 for deletion.
  • Right to opt out of sale or sharing: we do not sell or share personal information. There is nothing to opt out of.
  • Right to non-discrimination: we will not discriminate against you for exercising any privacy right.

14. Apple Platform Disclosures

CloudKit

  • Thresh writes Profile, Application, and Vouch records to the public CloudKit container, tagged with Apple-managed opaque iCloud identifiers.
  • CloudKit is operated by Apple; Apple's Privacy Policy and CloudKit security model apply.

StoreKit 2

  • Thresh uses StoreKit 2 for an auto-renewable subscription and a one-time lifetime purchase.
  • All transaction processing is handled by Apple. Kynoss Studios does not receive, store, or access payment information.

Push Notifications (APNs)

  • Thresh uses APNs for application and activity notifications. Push is opt-in and can be disabled at any time in iOS Settings.

Required Reason APIs & App Tracking Transparency

Thresh uses only Apple's first-party frameworks. Any Required Reason API usage is declared in the app's privacy manifest (PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy). Thresh does not track users across apps or websites owned by other companies, so the App Tracking Transparency framework is not used.
