Frequently Asked Questions

How does Layyr work?
Each day, Layyr presents a new puzzle with overlapping shapes stacked on top of each other. Your goal is to identify which shapes are present by examining the composite image. The fewer hints you need, the higher your score. Everyone gets the same puzzle each day.
Does Layyr collect any data or track me?
No. Layyr has no servers, no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no tracking. All game data (scores, streaks, statistics) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. The only external service is Game Center, which is managed entirely by Apple.
Does Layyr require an internet connection?
No. All puzzles are procedurally generated on your device using a daily seed, so gameplay works completely offline. Game Center leaderboard syncing requires a connection but is not required for gameplay.
What is the difference between free and Layyr Pro?
The free version gives you the full daily puzzle every day with shareable results. Layyr Pro ($2.99, one-time purchase) unlocks the puzzle archive (play any past puzzle), Game Center leaderboards, detailed statistics, practice mode, and visual themes. There are no subscriptions and no ads in either version.
When does the daily puzzle reset?
A new puzzle is available every day at midnight in your local time zone.
Can I replay today's puzzle?
Each daily puzzle can only be played once for scoring. Layyr Pro unlocks practice mode where you can play unlimited random puzzles without affecting your daily score.
How does Game Center work with Layyr?
If you're signed into Game Center and have Layyr Pro, your daily scores are automatically submitted to the daily leaderboard. You can view leaderboards and achievements through the Game Center button in the app. Game Center sign-in is optional.
What happens to my data if I delete the app?
All Layyr data is stored locally on your device. When you delete the app, all game data (scores, streaks, statistics) is permanently removed by iOS. Game Center achievements and leaderboard entries are retained by Apple in your Game Center profile and will reappear if you reinstall.
What iOS version is required?
Layyr requires iOS 26 or later and is designed for iPhone.
Is Layyr part of a game suite?
Yes! Layyr is part of the Kynoss Daily Games suite alongside Riffl (word puzzle), Paeki (image recognition), and Intuii (estimation). Each game tests a different cognitive skill with the same philosophy: one puzzle a day, no ads, no tracking.
