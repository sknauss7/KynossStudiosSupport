Riffl

Unshuffle two words. One puzzle a day.

How to Play

Each day, two words are shuffled together into a single row of letters. Tap each letter to assign it to Word A or Word B. The goal is to split them back into the two correct words with as few reassignments as possible.

  • Tap a letter once to assign it to Word A (blue).
  • Tap again to move it to Word B (orange).
  • Tap once more to unassign it.
  • When every letter is assigned and both words are correct, you win!

Scoring

Your score is based on how many times you reassign letters:

  • 0 reassignments — ⭐⭐⭐ Perfect
  • 1–2 reassignments — ⭐⭐ Great
  • 3–5 reassignments — ⭐ Good
  • 6+ reassignments — Solved

Daily Puzzle

Everyone gets the same puzzle each day. A new puzzle is available every day at midnight (your local time). Your progress is saved automatically if you leave mid-game.

Streaks & Stats

Riffl tracks your current streak, longest streak, games played, and star distribution — all stored locally on your device.

Sharing Results

After solving, you can share your result as a spoiler-free emoji card. No words are revealed — just your star rating and a color pattern.

Accessibility

Riffl supports VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, and Reduce Motion. If you encounter any accessibility issues, please reach out.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account?
No. There are no accounts, logins, or sign-ups.

Does Riffl work offline?
Yes. The app works entirely offline with no network connection required.

Can I get hints?
There are no hints by design. Every puzzle is solvable — take your time!

How do I reset my stats?
Deleting and reinstalling the app will reset all data.
