Frequently Asked Questions

How does Anomii work?
Each day, Anomii presents four rounds of puzzles. In each round, you see a 4×4 grid of 16 items (words, numbers, or symbols). Fifteen items follow a hidden rule — one does not. Tap the item you believe is the anomaly. You get one tap per round, and difficulty escalates from easy to tricky across the four rounds. Everyone gets the same puzzles each day.
What kinds of patterns does Anomii use?
Patterns span multiple categories: numeric (divisibility, sequences, primes), linguistic (syllable count, letter patterns), semantic (category membership, compound words), symbolic (shapes, sequences), and logical (multi-attribute rules). Round 1 uses simple patterns; Round 4 uses ambiguous patterns where multiple rules seem plausible but only one has exactly one exception.
Does Anomii collect any data or track me?
No. Anomii has no servers, no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no tracking. All game data (scores, streaks, statistics) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. The only external service is Game Center, which is managed entirely by Apple.
Does Anomii require an internet connection?
No. All puzzles are generated on-device from a date seed — no download required. You can play completely offline. Game Center leaderboard syncing requires a connection but is not required for gameplay.
What is the difference between free and Anomii Pro?
The free version gives you the full daily puzzle (all four rounds) every day with Game Center leaderboards and shareable results. Anomii Pro ($2.99, one-time purchase) unlocks the puzzle archive (play any past day), practice mode (unlimited random puzzles), detailed statistics by pattern category, and a streak freeze bank. There are no subscriptions and no ads in either version.
When does the daily puzzle reset?
A new puzzle is available every day at midnight in your local time zone.
Can I replay today's puzzle?
Each daily puzzle can only be played once for leaderboard scoring. Anomii Pro unlocks practice mode where you can play unlimited random puzzles, and the archive where you can replay any past daily puzzle.
How do streaks work?
Your streak counts consecutive days where you correctly identify at least 3 out of 4 anomalies. Anomii Pro includes a streak freeze bank — earned through play, not purchased — that protects your streak if you miss a day.
How does Game Center work with Anomii?
If you're signed into Game Center, your daily scores are automatically submitted to the daily leaderboard. You can view leaderboards and achievements through the Game Center button in the app. Game Center sign-in is optional — you can play without it.
What happens to my data if I delete the app?
All Anomii data is stored locally on your device. When you delete the app, all game data (scores, streaks, statistics) is permanently removed by iOS. Game Center achievements and leaderboard entries are retained by Apple in your Game Center profile and will reappear if you reinstall.
What iOS version is required?
Anomii requires iOS 18 or later and is designed for iPhone.
