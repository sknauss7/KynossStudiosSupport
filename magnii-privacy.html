1. Overview

Magnii ("the App") is developed and published by Kynoss Studios ("we," "us," or "our"). Magnii is a smart loupe and inspection tool for iPhone (iOS 18.1 and later) that combines magnification, text recognition, measurement, annotation, and PDF report export.

This Privacy Policy explains what data Magnii accesses, how that data is processed, and your rights regarding your information. We are committed to transparency and to protecting your privacy.

Magnii operates entirely on your device. We do not operate servers, databases, or cloud services. We do not create user accounts. We have no mechanism to collect, receive, view, or store your personal data. The data Magnii works with exists solely on your iPhone, under your control.

2. Camera Access

Magnii never uploads, transmits, or shares your camera feed or captured images.

Your camera is used for live magnification and image capture. All captured images remain on your device.

Why Camera Access Is Needed

Magnii uses your iPhone's camera for live magnification viewing and to capture still images for inspection. Camera access is required for the app's core functionality.

How Camera Data Is Used

  • The live camera feed is displayed for real-time magnification — it is never recorded or stored.
  • When you capture an image, it is saved locally within the app's inspection session.
  • Captured images are processed on-device for text recognition (OCR) and measurement using Apple's Vision framework.
  • No camera data is ever sent to any server, API, or external service.

What We Store

Data Stored Purpose Transmitted Off Device?
Captured inspection images Reference images for inspection sessions No
Extracted text (OCR results) Part markings, serial numbers, fine print No

Your Control

Camera access requires your explicit permission via the iOS permission prompt. You can revoke access at any time in Settings > Magnii. Without camera access, you can still view and export previously captured inspection sessions.

3. On-Device AI Processing

Magnii uses Apple's built-in frameworks for all AI and machine learning features:

  • Vision framework: Performs text recognition (OCR) on captured images to extract readable text like serial numbers, part markings, and fine print. Also detects reference objects (calibration cards) for measurement. All processing runs on your device's Neural Engine.
  • Vision framework (classification): Identifies objects in captured images to help you understand what you're looking at. This runs entirely on your device.
  • Foundation Models framework (optional): When available on supported devices, provides intelligent text-based assistance such as interpreting OCR results or suggesting what a part number belongs to. This model runs entirely on your device — no data is sent to any server, including Apple's.

No AI processing involves any external service, API call, or network request. All intelligence is computed locally using Apple's on-device frameworks.

4. Data Storage & On-Device Processing

All data processing occurs entirely on your iPhone. Magnii has no servers, no cloud infrastructure, no databases, and no backend of any kind. We literally cannot access your data because we have no system to receive it.

Inspection session data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is protected by:

  • iOS Data Protection (encrypted at rest, tied to your device passcode)
  • App sandboxing (only Magnii can access its own data store)
  • Standard iOS security architecture

Magnii does not use:

  • iCloud or any cloud storage
  • Any third-party SDKs, frameworks, or libraries
  • Analytics services (no Firebase, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude, etc.)
  • Advertising SDKs or ad networks
  • Crash reporting services
  • Any form of user tracking or fingerprinting

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Retention Policy

  • Inspection sessions (images, annotations, measurements, text) are retained on your device until you delete them or delete the app.
  • Generated PDF reports are created on-demand and shared only when you explicitly use the share sheet.

Deletion

  • Delete individual sessions: You can delete any inspection session from within the app.
  • Delete the app: Deleting Magnii from your device permanently removes all inspection data, captured images, and settings.
  • There is no server-side data to delete, no account to close, and no request process needed. You have full and immediate control.

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

Magnii does not share your data with anyone.

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your data with third parties.
  • We do not transmit your data off your device.
  • We do not use third-party SDKs that could collect data.
  • We do not serve advertisements.
  • We do not engage in data mining or user profiling.
  • We do not share data with parent companies, subsidiaries, or related entities (Kynoss Studios is an independent sole developer).

Magnii makes no network requests whatsoever. The app functions entirely offline. The only exception is StoreKit 2 for processing your one-time Pro purchase, which is handled entirely by Apple — we never see your payment information.

7. Data Security

Magnii relies on Apple's built-in security infrastructure to protect your data:

  • Encryption at rest: All local data is protected by iOS Data Protection, which encrypts data using keys tied to your device passcode.
  • App sandboxing: iOS prevents other apps from accessing Magnii's data store.
  • No network exposure: Because Magnii does not transmit any data over any network, there is no risk of data interception, server breaches, or unauthorized remote access.
  • No accounts: There are no credentials to steal, no passwords to breach, and no accounts to compromise.

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Magnii is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Because Magnii does not collect, transmit, or store personal information on any server, and because all data remains solely on the user's device, Magnii does not create the data collection risks that COPPA is designed to address. Nonetheless:

  • Magnii does not collect personal information from any user, including children.
  • Magnii does not require or support account creation.
  • Magnii contains no advertising or social features.
  • Magnii does not transmit any data to Kynoss Studios or any third party.

If you are a parent or guardian and have questions about this policy, please contact us at kynossstudios@gmail.com.

9. Your Rights Under GDPR (European Economic Area)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) provides you with specific rights regarding your personal data. We respect these rights, even though Magnii's on-device architecture means we do not act as a data controller or processor of your personal data in the traditional sense — because your data never reaches us.

Legal Basis for Processing

To the extent any on-device processing constitutes "processing" under the GDPR, the legal basis is:

  • Consent: You explicitly grant permission for camera access through the iOS permission prompt. You may withdraw consent at any time via iOS Settings.
  • Legitimate interest: Processing your data on your own device to provide the app's functionality directly to you.

Your Rights

  • Right of access: All data is stored on your device and accessible to you at all times within the app.
  • Right to rectification: You can edit or re-capture any inspection session.
  • Right to erasure: You can delete individual sessions or all data by deleting the app.
  • Right to restrict processing: You can revoke camera permission at any time without affecting access to existing sessions.
  • Right to data portability: You can export any session as a PDF report and share it via the standard iOS share sheet.
  • Right to object: You can stop all data processing by revoking permissions or deleting the app.

Data Transfers

Magnii does not transfer personal data outside your device. No cross-border data transfers occur because no data is transmitted to any server, anywhere.

Data Protection Officer

Due to the nature of our data processing (entirely on-device, no data collection), we are not required to appoint a Data Protection Officer. For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at kynossstudios@gmail.com.

10. Your Rights Under CCPA/CPRA (California)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) provides you with specific rights.

Information We Collect

Under CCPA/CPRA definitions, Magnii processes the following categories of information on your device only:

  • Camera images (captured for on-device inspection, never transmitted)

However, because all processing occurs on your device and we never collect, receive, or access this information, Kynoss Studios does not "collect" personal information as defined by the CCPA/CPRA.

Your Rights

  • Right to know: This Privacy Policy describes all data the app processes. We do not collect additional data beyond what is described here.
  • Right to delete: Delete individual sessions in-app or delete the app to remove all data.
  • Right to opt out of sale: We do not sell your personal information. We never have and never will.
  • Right to opt out of sharing: We do not share your personal information with third parties for cross-context behavioral advertising or any other purpose.
  • Right to non-discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your privacy rights.

"Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information"

Magnii does not sell or share personal information. Because no data is transmitted from your device to us or any third party, there is no sale or sharing to opt out of. We fully comply with the CCPA/CPRA without requiring any user action.
