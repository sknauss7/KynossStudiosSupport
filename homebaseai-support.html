Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save items?
Just tell HomeBaseAI in conversation. Type "I have a Samsung fridge in the kitchen," point your camera at a product label, or tap the microphone and describe what you see. The AI extracts the details and saves everything automatically.
What is the Home Health Score?
A number from 0 to 100 that shows how well-maintained your home is, based on maintenance compliance, overdue tasks, coverage, and documentation. It updates weekly. Available with HomeBase Pro.
What is HomeBase Pro?
Pro adds proactive maintenance coaching, home health score, seasonal prep checklists, cost-of-neglect tracking, and premium widgets. $5.99/month or $49.99/year with a 7-day free trial. The free tier is a complete home vault with unlimited items, rooms, scanning, voice input, and family sharing.
How does Home Setup work?
Say "Home Setup" in the conversation and HomeBaseAI walks you through your major systems one by one — HVAC, water heater, appliances, safety equipment. Scan labels, skip what you don't have, pause and resume anytime. Maintenance reminders are created automatically.
What are the AI features?
On-device AI powered by Apple Intelligence handles conversational intent classification, data extraction from labels, maintenance coaching, and natural language queries. All processing happens entirely on your iPhone.
Does HomeBaseAI work without Apple Intelligence?
Yes. On devices that don't support Apple Intelligence, a keyword-based fallback provides basic save and search functionality. The camera still works for OCR scanning.
Does HomeBaseAI send my data anywhere?
No. All data is stored on your device. If you enable family sharing, home data syncs through your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit — encrypted and controlled by you. We have no servers, no accounts, and no way to see your data.
How does family sharing work?
You can invite family members to share a home via iCloud. Everyone can see and update the same items, rooms, and maintenance tasks. Sharing uses Apple's CloudKit — data syncs through your iCloud accounts, not through our servers.
What happens if I delete the app?
All local data is permanently removed. Shared data in iCloud persists for other family members who still have the app. There is nothing to delete from our servers because we don't have any.
How do I manage my subscription?
Go to Settings on your iPhone > your Apple ID > Subscriptions > HomeBase Pro. You can also tap "Manage Subscription" in the app's Settings screen.
What iOS version is required?
HomeBaseAI requires iOS 26 or later. AI features including maintenance coaching work on Apple Intelligence-capable devices.
