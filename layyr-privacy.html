1. Overview

Layyr ("the App") is developed and published by Kynoss Studios ("we," "us," or "our"). Layyr is a daily visual puzzle game for iPhone (iOS 26 and later) where players identify shapes hidden within overlapping transparent layers.

This Privacy Policy explains what data Layyr accesses, how that data is processed, and your rights regarding your information.

Layyr operates entirely on your device. We do not operate servers, databases, or cloud services. We do not create user accounts. We have no mechanism to collect, receive, view, or store your personal data.

2. Game Data

All game data stays on your device. Layyr stores puzzle progress, scores, streaks, and statistics locally. None of this data is transmitted to Kynoss Studios or any third party.

3. Game Center

Layyr integrates with Apple's Game Center for leaderboards and achievements. Game Center is managed entirely by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

  • What Layyr sends to Game Center: Your daily puzzle score and achievement completions. Nothing else.
  • Game Center is optional. You can play Layyr without signing into Game Center. If you choose not to sign in, no data is sent to Game Center.
  • Game Center leaderboards require Layyr Pro. Free users do not submit scores to leaderboards.
  • Game Center data is managed by Apple. Kynoss Studios does not have access to your Game Center profile, friends list, or any data Apple stores.

4. Data Storage & On-Device Processing

All data processing occurs entirely on your iPhone. Layyr has no servers, no cloud infrastructure, no databases, and no backend of any kind.

Game data is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework, protected by:

  • iOS Data Protection (encrypted at rest, tied to your device passcode)
  • App sandboxing (only Layyr can access its own data store)
  • Standard iOS security architecture

Layyr does not use:

  • iCloud or any cloud storage
  • Any third-party SDKs, frameworks, or libraries
  • Analytics services (no Firebase, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude, etc.)
  • Advertising SDKs or ad networks
  • Crash reporting services
  • Any form of user tracking or fingerprinting

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Retention Policy

  • Game data is retained on your device until you delete the app.
  • Game Center data is retained by Apple according to Apple's data retention policies.

Deletion

  • Delete the app: Deleting Layyr permanently removes all local game data. Game Center entries are retained by Apple.
  • There is no server-side data to delete, no account to close, and no request process needed.

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

Layyr does not share your data with anyone.

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your data with third parties.
  • We do not transmit your data off your device (except scores to Game Center, if you opt in).
  • We do not use third-party SDKs that could collect data.
  • We do not serve advertisements.
  • We do not engage in data mining or user profiling.

7. Data Security

Layyr relies on Apple's built-in security infrastructure:

  • Encryption at rest: Protected by iOS Data Protection.
  • App sandboxing: iOS prevents other apps from accessing Layyr's data.
  • No network exposure: No data interception, server breaches, or unauthorized remote access possible.
  • No accounts: No credentials to steal, no passwords to breach.

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Layyr is a puzzle game rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Because Layyr does not collect or transmit personal information, it does not create the data collection risks COPPA addresses.

If you are a parent or guardian and have questions, please contact us at kynossstudios@gmail.com.

9. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are in the EEA, the GDPR provides you with specific rights. Because your data never reaches us, we do not act as a data controller or processor in the traditional sense.

  • Right of access: All data is on your device and accessible within the app.
  • Right to erasure: Delete the app to remove all data.
  • Right to restrict processing: Decline Game Center sign-in to prevent any score data from leaving your device.

No cross-border data transfers by Kynoss Studios occur. Contact: kynossstudios@gmail.com.

10. Your Rights Under CCPA/CPRA

If you are a California resident:

  • Right to know: This policy describes all data the app processes.
  • Right to delete: Delete the app to remove all local data.
  • Right to opt out of sale: We do not sell your personal information.
  • Right to opt out of sharing: We do not share your personal information with third parties.
  • Right to non-discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

11. Apple Platform Disclosures

Game Center

  • Layyr uses GameKit to submit scores to leaderboards and report achievements, only when signed in and using Pro features.
  • Game Center is optional. Core gameplay does not require it.

StoreKit 2 (In-App Purchases)

  • Layyr uses StoreKit 2 to offer a one-time "Layyr Pro" in-app purchase.
  • All transaction processing is handled by Apple. Kynoss Studios does not receive or store payment information.

App Tracking Transparency

Layyr does not track users across apps or websites. ATT is not used because no tracking occurs.
