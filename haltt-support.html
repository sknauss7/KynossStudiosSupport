Frequently Asked Questions

Does Haltt upload my data anywhere?
No. Haltt processes everything entirely on your device using Apple's Vision and Foundation Models frameworks. No screenshots, purchase data, or personal information are ever uploaded, transmitted, or shared. We have no servers to receive them.
How does screenshot analysis work?
When you share or import a screenshot, Haltt uses Apple's Vision framework to read text from the image (product names, prices) directly on your device. Then Apple's on-device AI (Foundation Models) classifies the purchase as a want or need. No data leaves your iPhone at any point in this process.
Does Haltt need access to my photo library?
No. Haltt only processes screenshots you explicitly share with it — either through the Share Sheet extension or by importing from the app. Haltt never scans or accesses your photo library in the background.
What is the cooling-off timer?
When you log an impulse purchase temptation, Haltt starts a configurable timer (you choose the duration). When the timer expires, you'll get a notification asking if you still want the item. If you resisted, the amount is added to your savings total. The idea is simple: most impulse urges fade if you just wait.
What is the difference between free and Pro?
The free version includes manual item entry, cooling-off timers, basic want-vs-need scoring, and a savings counter for up to 3 active items. The one-time $4.99 Pro upgrade unlocks screenshot capture with automatic OCR, AI-powered analysis, the Share Sheet extension, unlimited active items, the Money Saved widget, full gamification (streaks, achievements), and spending pattern insights.
Does Haltt work without an internet connection?
Yes. All features work entirely offline. Haltt makes no network requests.
What happens to my data if I delete the app?
All Haltt data is stored locally on your device. When you delete the app, all associated data is permanently removed by iOS. There is nothing to delete from any server because no data is ever transmitted off your device.
Does the AI give financial advice?
No. Haltt's AI categorizes purchases as wants or needs to help you reflect on your spending habits. It does not provide financial advice, investment guidance, or personalized financial recommendations. Think of it as a mirror, not an advisor.
What iOS version is required?
Haltt requires iOS 26 or later and is designed for iPhone. AI-powered analysis requires a device with Apple Intelligence support (iPhone 15 Pro or later). The free tier's basic scoring works on all iOS 26 devices.
Can I export my savings data?
Yes. Pro users can export their purchase history and savings data from within the app.
